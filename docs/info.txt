Technique 3 — Hypothesis Testing
This is where many students become generic. You should make it operational.
Strong Hypothesis Ideas
Hypothesis 1
H₀:
Government and private-sector projects have equal average payment delays.
H₁:
Government projects have significantly longer payment delays.
Use:
independent t-test.
Business implication:
working capital strain,
financing cost impact.

Hypothesis 2
H₀:
Project profitability does not differ by project type.
H₁:
Profitability differs significantly across project categories.
Use:
ANOVA.
Business implication:
strategic bidding decisions.
